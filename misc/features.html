..Cars..

Hatchback:

Hatchbacks are small to medium-sized cars with a rear door that swings upward like a hatch. They are easy to drive in the city and offer flexible cargo space, as the back seats can usually be folded down to create more room for luggage or shopping.

Sedan:

Sedans have a traditional design with three distinct sections: the engine bay, the passenger cabin, and a separate trunk at the back. They are known for providing a smooth and comfortable ride, making them a popular choice for families.

Coupe:

A coupe is a stylish and sporty car, generally with two doors and a sleek, sloping roofline. Coupes focus on a sporty appearance and driving performance, which makes them attractive to car enthusiasts.

Convertible:

Convertibles are cars with a roof that can be folded down or completely removed. They offer the thrill of open-air driving and are often sporty and eye-catching, perfect for pleasant weather and scenic drives.

SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle):

SUVs are larger vehicles with higher ground clearance and a spacious interior. They can handle rough roads and challenging terrain, making them suitable for both city commutes and adventurous trips with family or friends.

Pickup Truck:

Pickup trucks have an enclosed cabin for passengers and an open cargo bed at the back for carrying goods and equipment. They are practical for work purposes, outdoor activities, and transporting heavy or bulky items.

Minivan / MPV (Multi-Purpose Vehicle):

Minivans or MPVs are designed to carry more passengers comfortably. They typically seat seven or more people and often have sliding side doors and flexible seating arrangements, making them ideal for large families and long road trips.
