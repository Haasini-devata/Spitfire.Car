ICE Cars

Vehicles powered by Internal Combustion Engines, which are the traditional gasoline or diesel-powered cars. These engines burn fuel within the engine to produce the energy that moves the vehicle.

Electric Cars

These are powered by electricity stored in a battery, using an electric motor for propulsion instead of a gasoline engine. They offer several benefits, including reduced emissions, lower maintenance costs, and potentially lower running costs.

Hybrid Cars

Combine a traditional combustion engine with an electric motor and battery system, offering improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions compared to conventional vehicles.
